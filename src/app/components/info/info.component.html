What is Aggregatr?

Aggregatr helps you organize the music you love on Spotify

No more having to scroll through hundreds of songs in your library to find that song you saved from months ago
Aggregatr generates playlists based on the date that you saved a track - neatly organizing them into chronological units
Just specify the month and year, and a new playlist will be created for you

More Features Coming Soon:
Custom date filters, ability to remove and rearrange tracks in your aggregated lists, and more to come...
