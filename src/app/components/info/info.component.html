What is Aggregatr?

Aggregatr is the solution to organizing the music you love on Spotify.

No more having to scroll through hundreds of songs in your library to find that song you saved from months ago
Aggreagtr generates playlists based on the month you saved a track - neatly organizing them into chronological units
Just specify the month and year, and a new playlist will be created for you
